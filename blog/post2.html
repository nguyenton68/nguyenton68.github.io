Start a career in tech for a woman at beginning of 30?

Posted by Nguyen Ton on August 5, 2018

I have been confusing myself and thinking a lot about my career path recently when my 29th birthday is coming in 2 days.

Why it is so hard for a woman? At this age, you suppose to have baby (high risk if you have baby after 35) and have a family. For me, at this age, I don’t have anything because after I graduated from undergrad, I started to do my PhD, and till now, I’m still working on my PhD. This PhD ruined my life. I have no job, no family and I lost most beautiful time of a girl (twentysomething) just to dedicate my time for this SHIT PHD.

For a woman, after finishing a PhD, she will be around ~30, yah it’s too late. And it even harder if I switch from science track to technology track. Because the skill sets I obtained from PhD, only small part of it is needed in industry.

I have been practicing coding and learning about algorithm, python for data science, etc … but how can I compete with other candidates in computer science major?

This path is going to be hard, and it’s really hard, seriously.

I’m working on that
